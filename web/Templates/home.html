Who We Are

The Middlebury Student Investment Committee (SIC) is a student-run organization that actively manages a portion of Middlebury College's endowment. Founded in 2011, our committee consists of dedicated students passionate about finance, economics, and investment management.

Our mission is to preserve and grow capital while providing members with valuable hands-on experience in investment research and portfolio management. We employ a disciplined, research-driven approach focusing on long-term value creation.
